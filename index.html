GEMSTONE
CRM SOFTWARE

 CRM systems have grown steadily since their inception in the 1990s into robust, powerful and essential tools that most businesses depend on today.
  
This is especially true of the Gemstone software with its integrated advertising system which includes audience analysis enabling staff to reclaim customer engagement.
 
Used correctly and to its full potential, Gemstone gives you the power to grow digital, mobile and print revenues. Put simply, it gives you the tools to provide intelligent campaign proposals to maximise every customer's marketing requirement.
   
This module is designed to give you the core basics of Gemstone functionality. Upon completion you will be able to:
  • Search for specific customers on the database
  • See at a glance a customer's contact history, activity history and account information
  • Understand how to read and apply a customer's GDPR settings
  • Add notes and attachments to a customer's "Journal"
  • Edit a contact record
  • Create a new contact record
  • Record you own customer activity and create follow-up "Tasks"
  • Make a total audience booking (digital and print) using the Multi-media Rate Card (MMRC)

Keeping tabs on your customer

The benefits of keeping records

    Gemstone's contact records contain so much more than just addresses and telephone numbers. Each one also gives a full history of our interraction
    with the customer; every call, email and advert. Reviewing this helps you to remember key service and personal details presenting yourself as a true professional with understanding of;

  • Products they’ve previously booked
  • Their needs, USP, history and reactions
  • The types of campaigns that have worked effectively for them
  • Their typical spend and advertising pattern
